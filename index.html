Harry Potter

The Harry Potter film series is a British film series based on the first seven Harry Potter books by the English author J.K. Rowling. The series started in 2001 and was completed in 2011 and consists of eight feature films. The last book, the seventh, was made into a film in two parts. Since 2016, the series has been supplemented with several prequels, which are based on the book Fantastic Animals and Where to Find Them.
